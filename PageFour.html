Pacman Frog

In case you are wondering what the interesting thing with the
Pacman frog is all about, then this is your chance to find out.

  • Pacman frogs are of medium to large size, with an adult-measured average length of about 4–7 inches.
  • It also gave them their other popular name: ‘Pac-Man’
    due to their oval-shaped, stocky bodies and big mouths
    full of sharp teeth.
  • Pacman frogs are native to grasslands and savannas
    with soft, loamy soil and a few wells.
