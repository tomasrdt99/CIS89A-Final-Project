General Facts

On this page, we'll be going over some generic facts about frogs
that you may or may not know. I hope you enjoy learning!

  • There is evidence that frogs have roamed the Earth for
    more than 200 million years, as long as the dinosaurs.
  • The world's largest frog is the goliath frog of West Africa
    —it can grow to 15 inches and weigh up to 7 pounds.
  • One of the smallest is the Cuban tree toad, which
    grows to half an inch long.
